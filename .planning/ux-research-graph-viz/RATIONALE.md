# Pick: @cosmos.gl/graph (MIT core of Cosmograph)

**@cosmograph/react is license-blocked (CC-BY-NC-4.0), so we wrap the upstream MIT engine @cosmos.gl/graph directly — this gives the same premium WebGL rendering feel Cosmograph is known for, with no commercial restriction, 1M-node headroom, and enough surface (setPointColors / setPointSizes / hovered+focused rings / curved links) to port our entire slice-3 polish without rewriting the renderer.**
