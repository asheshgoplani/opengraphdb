- **WHOIS privacy**: Free, automatic

## Research Summary

Researched pricing across 8 registrars, community opinions on Reddit/HackerNews/blogs (100+ sources, 7 parallel research agents). Key findings:

- Cloudflare was cheapest at $70/yr (at-cost). Namecheap was $80/yr with $93/yr renewals. GoDaddy worst at $100+ with 45% renewal hikes.
- Porkbun was the community favorite for experience, Cloudflare for price. We went with Cloudflare.
- Locked in before the March 5, 2026 wholesale price increase ($10/yr hike).

## Open Items

- **Consider securing opengraphdb.com** defensively for brand protection
- **Name collision risk**: "Open Graph" overlaps with Facebook's Open Graph Protocol (og:tags) in developer mindshare. Worth evaluating a rebrand before public v0.1 launch. Alternative names explored: graphite.ai, arcgraph.ai, nodara.ai, grale.ai, lattiq.ai.
- **Email deliverability**: .ai domains have reported spam filter issues. Consider using a .com email address for transactional mail.
